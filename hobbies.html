MY HOBBIES

Not only do I like the break from the monotonous rountine of school and homework, but I am always up to try new activites and learn more about the world. This trip will be a memory that I will always cherish. All the long hikes and beautiful scenery will always be saved in my mind. No matter the place, I am always up for travel.

Tennis

I have been playing tennis since I was 5 years old. 10 years later, it is still my favorite sport out of the many I have tried. I remember the first time I picked up a tennis racket and stepped onto the court. Over the years of playing tennis, I have made many memories with my coaches, friends, and family. The experiences that I have in tennis are extraordinary and unforgettable, and if I could do it all over again, I would choose tennis all over again. I remember as a little kid watching Nadal, Djokovic, and Federer compete against each other in the huge tounrnaments. I was always fond of their fun competitive spirit, and one day I hope to go in person and watch history be made at the US Open. Though the big 3 may not be playing, Tennis is a sport I will remember forever.
